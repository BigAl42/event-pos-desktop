# Void

**When?** A line or whole receipt was **posted incorrectly** and should be reversed (void).

**Steps**

1. Home → **Void**.
2. **Recent receipts** on the left – pick a receipt.
3. **Lines** for that receipt appear on the right.
4. Either:
   - **Void** per line, or
   - **Void entire receipt** (all non-voided lines at once).

## Notes

- **Recorded on** shows which **register** created the receipt.
- **Void entire receipt** opens a **warning** – confirm only when sure.
- Voided lines are marked **voided** and cannot be voided again.

## Sync

Voids sync like postings. After a void, registers may take a moment to match.

## Common issues

- **No receipts** → none in the active cycle yet.
- **Error when voiding** → check network/sync ([Sync status](handbuch://sync-status)); retry.
